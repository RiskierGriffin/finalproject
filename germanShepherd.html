About

The German Shepherd is a large sized breed belonging to the working dog class. It is highly intelligent an versatile. With an average length of 2 feet, it is an agile and powerful dog, known for its high maintenence. It requires a lot of physical activity and is likely to tire out the owner long before it is exhausted.
