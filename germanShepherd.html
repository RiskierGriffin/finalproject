About

The German Shepherd is a large sized breed belonging to the working dog class. It is highly intelligent and versatile. With an average length of 2 feet, it is an agile and powerful dog, known for its high maintenence. It requires a lot of physical activity and is likely to tire out the owner long before it is exhausted.

Versatility

While German Shepherds are owned as household pets, their versatility also makes them a popular choice in anything from being a service dog to military and police work.

Hair/Fur

A German Shepherd is double-coated. It requires a lot of grooming and gets dirty very easy.
